Biography

Trevor Noah is a South African comedian, writer, producer, political commentator, actor, and television host. Born in Johannesburg, South Africa, in 1984, he rose to international fame as the host of *The Daily Show* on Comedy Central. Known for his sharp wit and insightful social commentary, Trevor has used humor to explore complex issues such as race, politics, and identity. His journey from apartheid-era South Africa to becoming one of the most influential voices in comedy is a testament to his resilience, talent, and unique perspective.
